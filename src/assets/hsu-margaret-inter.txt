The rivalry between China and the United States has influenced many aspects of the world, from trade and technology to international relations. Since both countries are major global powers, their actions often affect economies, businesses, and even the policies of smaller countries.
Personally, this rivalry has helped me understand how connected the world really is. As a student from Myanmar studying abroad, I have seen how decisions made by powerful countries can create both opportunities and challenges for developing nations. Whether it is investment, trade, education, or technology, the effects can be felt even in countries that are not directly involved in the competition.While competition between major powers can sometimes create tensions, it can also encourage innovation and progress if it is managed responsibly.
